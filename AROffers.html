AR Offers Documentation

Introduction

Overview

This section provides an overview of the AR Offer feature. Learn about how users can explore offers from different developers and view specific developer profiles focusing on their listed properties with offers.

Exploring AR Offers

To explore AR Offers, follow these steps:

  1. From the header, find and click on the "AR Offer" button.
  2. A pop-up will appear, displaying a list of developers along with their respective offers.

Developer Offers Profile

Profile Overview

Clicking on a specific developer from the AR Offer pop-up will lead you to the developer's profile page, focusing specifically on properties with offers.

  • Developer Information: Basic details about the developer.
  • Developer Offers: A list of properties associated with the developer that have active offers.

Listing with Offers

The developer's profile page will showcase a chronological list of properties with active offers. This list is ordered from the most recent to the oldest offers.

  • Listing Details: Each listing includes information such as property name, type, status, and details about the offer.
  • Sort Order: The listings are arranged in descending order, starting with the most recent offers.
