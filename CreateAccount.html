Create Account Documentation

Introduction

Welcome to our website! Follow the steps below to create your account and get started.

Steps to Signup

  1. Visit our website at AR-Realstate.com

  2. Click on the "Signup" button or navigate to the sign up page.

  3. Choose your user type from the dropdown: User, Developer, or Microdeveloper.

  4. Enter your desired username, email, and password in the respective fields.

  5. Click the "Signup" button to create your account.

  6. Upon successful sign up, you will be redirected to the login page.

  7. Use your credentials to log in and explore our platform!

Sign up Options

Options

In Sign in Page , click on the "Signup" button to navigate to the sign up Options popup.

User

If you choose User, you can use traditional signup or speed up the process by signing up with your Google account.

By Email

By Google Authorization

Developer

If you choose Developer, you will be directed to a dedicated developer signup page. Enter additional details about your company and projects.

Microdeveloper

If you choose Microdeveloper, follow specific steps to connect with an existing developer account. Learn how to add your details and skills.

Additional Information
