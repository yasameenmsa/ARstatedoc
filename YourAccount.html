Your Account Documentation

Updating Profile Information

1. Update Profile Image

To update your profile image, hover over your current profile picture. This will open a dialog where you can upload a new image. Ensure the new image meets the recommended dimensions for optimal display.

2. Update Username and Password

Navigate to your account by clicking on your profile picture. In the "Profile" tab, you'll find options to update your username and password. Enter the new information and confirm any password changes for security purposes. Click "Update" to apply the changes.

Create Listings

1. Navigate to Listings

Access the "Listings" section from the main navigation menu. This is where you can manage all your property listings.

2. Fill in Listing Details

Click on the "Add New Listing" button to start creating a new property listing. Fill in all the required details, such as property type, location, price, and additional information. Upload high-quality images to showcase the property effectively. You can also include a detailed property description. Once all details are entered, click "Submit" to add the listing to your profile.

3. Preview and Verify

Before finalizing the listing, take advantage of the preview option. This allows you to see how your listing will appear to potential buyers. Verify that all information is accurate and appealing. Make any necessary adjustments and then confirm the listing.

Editing Listings

1. Access Your Listings

To edit an existing listing, navigate to the "Listings" section and locate the property you want to edit. Click on the "Edit" or "Modify" option associated with the specific listing.

2. Make Changes

Update the necessary details, including property information, images, or any other relevant fields. You can also change the status of the listing (e.g., from "Active" to "Pending"). Once you've made the required changes, click "Save" or "Update" to apply the modifications.

3. Delete Listings

If you need to remove a listing, locate the "Delete" or "Remove" option in the listing editing interface. Confirm the deletion to permanently remove the listing from your profile.
